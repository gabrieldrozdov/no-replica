Highlights from the Impermanent Collection

Digital exhibition in the form of a video game

The RISD 2023 Graphic Design MFA Biennial, titled Highlights from the Impermanent Collection, featured an identity with the aesthetic of green jello. For the exhibition’s website, No Replica expanded on this identity and created a one-to-one digital recreation of the exhibition’s floor plan. The website places student artworks to match their real-world arrangement, including documentary photography from the live event. Visiting every tile of the digital exhibition reveals a secret prize!
