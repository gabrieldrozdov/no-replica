This Is For You

A handbook for design students

RISD’s Graphic Design MFA program culminates in the creation of a physical book that represents each student’s body of work and creative practice. This Is For You took a slightly different approach. Instead of centering the student creating the book, the thesis centered the book’s likely audience — prospective students looking for more insight into RISD’s program, and into a design career as a whole. To that end, This Is For You is an ongoing digital document chronicling Gabriel Drozdov’s life and career, with the intent of providing useful information to aspiring designers.
